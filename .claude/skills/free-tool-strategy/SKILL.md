## Ideation Framework

1. **What do your users need to calculate/analyze/generate?**
2. **What tools do they currently pay for?**
3. **What can you build in 1-2 weeks?**
4. **What has high search volume?**
5. **What leads naturally to your paid product?**

---

## Lead Capture Strategy

### Gating Approaches
| Approach | When to Use | Trade-off |
|----------|-------------|-----------|
| **No gate** | Brand awareness, SEO | More users, fewer leads |
| **Email gate** | High-value tools | Fewer users, more leads |
| **Partial gate** | Show preview, gate full | Balance |
| **Freemium** | Basic free, advanced paid | Conversion funnel |

### Best Practice
- Show value FIRST (no gate)
- Gate the DELIVERABLE (report, export, save)
- Not the INPUT (let them use it)

---

## Build vs Buy vs Embed

| Option | Pros | Cons |
|--------|------|------|
| **Build** | Full control, unique | Time, maintenance |
| **Buy** | Fast, proven | Less unique, cost |
| **Embed** | Easy, fast | Limited control |

## MVP Scope

Week 1: Core functionality
Week 2: Polish + lead capture
Week 3: Launch + promote

## Promotion Strategy

1. **SEO** - Target "[tool type] tool" keywords
2. **Social** - Share results, screenshots
3. **Communities** - Reddit, HN, forums
4. **Partnerships** - Embed in other products
5. **PR** - "We built this free tool"
