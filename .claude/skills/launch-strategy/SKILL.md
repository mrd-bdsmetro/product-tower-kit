# Launch Strategy — Product Launch

## Goal
Plan and execute product launches. From MVP to Product Hunt to scale.

---

## ORB Framework

| Channel | Type | Examples | Control |
|---------|------|----------|---------|
| **Owned** | You control | Website, email, blog, docs | Full |
| **Rented** | Platform controls | Twitter/X, LinkedIn, YouTube | Partial |
| **Borrowed** | Others control | Press, partnerships, affiliates | Low |

**Strategy:** Build Owned → Leverage Rented → Earn Borrowed

---

## 5-Phase Launch Approach

### Phase 1: Internal (Week 1)
- Team alignment
- Beta testing
- Bug fixes
- Documentation

### Phase 2: Alpha (Week 2-3)
- 10-50 users
- Collect feedback
- Iterate quickly
- Fix critical issues

### Phase 3: Beta (Week 4-6)
- 100-500 users
- Public beta
- Marketing prep
- Landing page live

### Phase 4: Early Access (Week 7-8)
- 500-1000 users
- Product Hunt prep
- Press outreach
- Launch assets ready

### Phase 5: Full Launch (Week 9-10)
- Product Hunt launch
- Press coverage
- Paid ads (optional)
- Scale marketing

---

## Product Hunt Strategy

### Before Launch
1. Build hunter profile (100+ upvotes on other products)
2. Create assets (logo, screenshots, video)
3. Write tagline (60 chars max)
4. Prepare maker comment
5. Line up supporters (50+ people)
6. Schedule for Tuesday-Thursday

### Launch Day
1. Post at 12:01 AM PST
2. Share link immediately
3. Respond to every comment
4. Share on Twitter/LinkedIn
5. Email your list
6. Post in relevant communities

### After Launch
1. Thank supporters
2. Share results
3. Follow up with leads
4. Iterate based on feedback
