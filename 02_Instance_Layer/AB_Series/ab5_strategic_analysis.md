## 1. Risk/Reward Asymmetric? (Naval)

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|------------|
| **Downside** | $0 (no investment, just time) |
| **Upside** | $9,900/year (100 users × $99) |

## 3. Antifragile? (Taleb)

**Question:** Does it get stronger when attacked?

| Attack Type | Response | Antifragile? |
|-------------|----------|--------------|

| Claude Code changes | Adapts to new platform | ⚠️ |
| Market shrinks | Pivots to new segment | ⚠️ |

**Verdict:** ⚠️ Moderately antifragile

- But: Platform dependency is a weakness
