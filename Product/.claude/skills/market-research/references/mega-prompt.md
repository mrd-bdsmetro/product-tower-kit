# Product Tower Kit — Mega Prompt for Deep Research

## Instructions

1. Copy this prompt
2. Replace [PLACEHOLDER] with your specifics
3. Paste into Google Deep Research, Grok Deep Search, or ChatGPT
4. Wait 2-10 minutes
5. Save output as `data/t0_deep_research.md`
6. Run: "parse deep research [file path]"

---

## Mega Prompt (8 Parts)

### Part 1: Market Size & Growth
```
What is the total addressable market (TAM), serviceable addressable market (SAM), and serviceable obtainable market (SOM) for [INDUSTRY] in [REGION]? Include:
- Current market size (2025-2026)
- Growth rate (CAGR)
- Projected size (2028-2030)
- Key drivers of growth
- Source: Reports from Mordor Intelligence, Statista, CBRE, Savills, government data
```

### Part 2: Target Segments
```
Identify 3-5 market segments for [PRODUCT/SERVICE] targeting [AUDIENCE]. For each segment:
- Demographic profile
- Behavioral patterns
- Pain points
- Willingness to pay
- Market size estimate
```

### Part 3: User Profiles
```
Create 2-4 detailed user personas for [PRODUCT/SERVICE]. For each persona:
- Name and background
- Daily routine and behaviors
- Goals and motivations
- Frustrations and pain points
- Current solutions they use
- Quote that captures their mindset
```

### Part 4: Needs & Pain Points
```
What are the top 10 needs and pain points of [TARGET AUDIENCE] when it comes to [ACTIVITY/PROBLEM]? For each:
- Description of need/pain
- Impact level (high/medium/low)
- Frequency (daily/weekly/monthly)
- Current solutions and their limitations
- Opportunity for improvement
```

### Part 5: Competitive Landscape
```
Analyze the competitive landscape for [PRODUCT/SERVICE] in [MARKET]. Include:
- Top 5-10 competitors (direct and indirect)
- Their strengths and weaknesses
- Pricing models
- Market positioning
- Gaps in the market
- Barriers to entry
```

### Part 6: Pricing Data
```
What pricing models and price points are used in [INDUSTRY] for [PRODUCT/SERVICE]? Include:
- Common pricing models (freemium, subscription, one-time, usage-based)
- Price ranges by segment
- Factors affecting pricing
- Willingness to pay data
- Price sensitivity analysis
```

### Part 7: Regulatory & Timing
```
What regulatory factors and timing catalysts affect [INDUSTRY] in [REGION]? Include:
- Key regulations
- Recent regulatory changes
- Upcoming changes
- Technology catalysts
- Market timing factors
- Why now is a good/bad time to enter
```

### Part 8: Trends & Opportunities
```
What are the major trends and emerging opportunities in [INDUSTRY]? Include:
- Technology trends
- Consumer behavior shifts
- Market gaps
- Emerging niches
- Future predictions (2-5 years)
- Risks and threats
```
