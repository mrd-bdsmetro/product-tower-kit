# Value Proposition

## Why Product Tower Kit Exists

Most solo founders waste 6-12 months building products nobody wants. They skip validation, ignore anti-bias testing, and fall in love with their first idea.

**Product Tower Kit exists to prevent this waste.**

## Core Value Proposition

> "Biến analysis thành evidence + execution"

Transform gut-feel ideas into validated products with measurable PMF scores.

## Problem-Solution Fit

### The Problem
| Pain Point | Impact |
|------------|--------|
| Founders build without validation | 70% of startups fail |
| No structured framework for validation | Adhoc decisions, missed gates |
| Confirmation bias blindsides | Build → Launch → Churn |
| Solo founder = no accountability | Procrastination, scope creep |

### Our Solution
| Feature | Benefit |
|---------|---------|
| 14-phase T-Series validation | Structured path from idea to PMF |
| 6-challenge AB anti-bias system | Stress-test assumptions before building |
| Quantitative PMF scoring | Know when to scale, when to pivot |
| Self-contained Product folder | No cross-kit pollution, easy to copy |

## Target Customer Profile

### Primary: Solo Founder with ADHD Brain
- Struggles with focus and follow-through
- Needs structure without bureaucracy
- Wants accountability without meetings

**Quote:** *"I need to validate my idea but I don't know where to start and I keep getting distracted."*

### Secondary: Early-Stage Indie Hacker
- Limited time and resources
- Needs to move fast with low risk
- Wants leverage from tools and frameworks

**Quote:** *"I want to know if my idea is worth pursuing before I spend 6 months building."*

## Differentiation

| Competitor | Their Approach | Our Approach |
|------------|---------------|--------------|
| **Lean Validation** | Generic framework | ADHD-friendly, VN market focus |
| **The Mom Test** | Qualitative only | Quantitative PMF scoring |
| **Y Combinator** | For startups | For pre-startup solo founders |
| **Notion Templates** | Tool-focused | Outcome-focused |

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | How Measured |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| Time to PMF | 60 days | From first T-tier to PMF score ≥ 30 |
| Validation completion | T0-T9.5 in 30 days | npm run harness:eval |
| PMF score | ≥ 30/50 | Automated calculation |

## Elevator Pitch

**For a solo founder who struggles with validation:**
"Product Tower Kit is a self-contained validation framework that helps ADHD founders like you turn ideas into evidence-backed products in 60 days, so you know exactly when to build and when to pivot."

## Tagline

**"Validate before you build."**
