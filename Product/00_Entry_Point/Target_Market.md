# Target Market

## Primary Target Market

**Solo founders in Vietnam** who want to validate their product ideas before investing significant time and resources.

### Demographics
| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Age** | 25-45 |
| **Location** | Vietnam (Ho Chi Minh City, Hanoi) |
| **Language** | Vietnamese, English |
| **Education** | University degree (technical or business) |
| **Income** | Middle-class, early career or career change |

### Psychographics
- Ambitious but overwhelmed by options
- Values efficiency and structure
- Concerned about failure and wasting time
- Seeks validation before committing
- Comfortable with technology and self-learning

## Secondary Target Markets

### Early-Stage Startups (2-3 people)
- Need structured validation process
- Limited budget for market research
- Want to impress investors with methodology

### Indie Hackers
- Remote, global, English-speaking
- Need fast, cheap validation
- Comfortable with CLI tools
- Building SaaS products

## Market Trends

### Supporting Trends
1. **Rise of solo founders** — Remote work enables one-person startups
2. **AI acceleration** — Faster building, but still need validation
3. **Vietnam startup ecosystem** — Growing demand for structured methodology
4. **ADHD awareness** — More founders recognize their needs

### Challenging Trends
1. **Information overload** — Too many frameworks, hard to choose
2. **Shiny object syndrome** — Founders chase new tools instead of validation
3. **Git-and-forget** — Frameworks get started but not finished
